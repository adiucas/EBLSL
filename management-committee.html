Chief Financial Officer
Mr. Md. Sayadur Rahman is the Managing Director of EBL Securities Limited. Mr. Rahman is also the running President of Bangladesh Merchant Bankers Association (BMBA). Mr. Rahman is a prominent capital market specialist of the country. He has around 36 years of professional experience in different capacities in different financial institutions like Insurance, Banking, Leasing, Merchant Banking and Brokerage etc. Mr. Rahman joined EBL in 2008 as Head of Investment Banking. In 2013, he took over as the Managing Director of EBL Securities Limited. He has completed his M.Com., MBA and LLM from different reputed universities. He is a widely traveled person and has special skills in networking & negotiation and special interest in CSR & social help.
Mr. M. Shahryar Faiz has more than 12 years of professional experience in investment research, portfolio management, credit risk management, operation and compliance. He is also a MANCOM member at EBLSL since 2017. He started his career with Standard Chartered Bank Bangladesh as a Risk Analyst.

Mr. Faiz joined EBL in 2009 as a Management Trainee Officer (MTO). Prior to taking over the current role, he was the Head of Research at EBL Securities Ltd. He also worked as the Head of Research at EBL Investments Ltd. and Sr. Investment Analyst in EBL’s Investment Banking Division. Mr. Faiz completed CFA Level-II from CFA Institute, USA, JAIBB from Institute of Bankers Bangladesh. Having a sound academic track record, Mr. Faiz obtained his MBA & BBA with Major in Finance from University of Dhaka. He attended many training programs, workshops, seminars and conferences on different areas throughout his career. He also an avid traveler and loves to travel and home and abroad during his free time.
Mr. Asaduzzaman is the Chief Financial Officer of EBL Securities Limited. He joined EBLSL in 2010 as Chief Financial Officer. With more than a decade of working history with EBLSL, Mr. Asaduzzaman has gained enormous knowledge and experience in the field business and operation of the company and currently overseeing the business team at EBLSL as an additional responsibility. Mr. Asad has around 14 years of professional experience in the accountancy profession. Prior to joining EBLSL, Mr. Asaduzzaman worked at Pacific Jeans Ltd. as Audit, Risk & Assurance Manager.

Mr. Asaduzzaman is a Certified Management Accountants from CMA, Australia. He has completed his Masters in International Economic Relations from University of Dhaka and Advanced Certificate in Business Administration (ACBA) from IBA, DU. He has pursued Chartered Accountancy course from KPMG (Rahman Rahman Huq). He is also pursuing Chartered Secretary Course from ICSB and successfully qualified CS-Inter (Level-3). He is also a qualified Income Tax Practitioner (ITP).

He attended various training in Capital Market related Laws, Bangladesh Financial Reporting Standard (BFRS), VAT & Taxes etc. both at home and abroad.
