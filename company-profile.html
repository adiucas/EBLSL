Company Profile

EBL Securities Limited (EBLSL) is fully owned subsidiary of Eastern Bank Ltd. EBL Securities Ltd. obtained the license from Bangladesh Securities and Exchange Commission (BSEC) as Stock Broker & Stock Dealer and is fully engaged in securities trading for the investors. The company holds the TREC of Dhaka Stock Exchange Limited (TREC No. 026) and Chittagong Stock Exchange Limited (TREC No. 021). It provides brokerage services through both DSE and CSE. The company also holds the Depository Participant (DP) license.

At present the company has two branches i.e., Karwan Bazar, Dhaka & Chittagong and one Extension Office at Motijheel. EBLSL has implemented the most updated software "Blue-chip" that can be maneuvered for all services needed to be provided by a brokerage house.

EBL Securities Ltd. is committed to being a preferred brokerage house providing comprehensive services to help achieve the financial goals through the guidance of its highly qualified and experienced human resources. EBLSL handles prestigious and renowned corporate clients, individual investors and financial institutions by providing a wide range of services.
