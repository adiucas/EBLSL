Bangladesh Securities and Exchange Commission (BSEC) has taken necessary steps in order to launch Nationwide Financial Literacy Program. The time frame of the program has been divided into 3 stages, i.e. short term, midterm and long term. Programs for different target groups has also been included in different time frames. As a stakeholder of BSEC and for its market development initiatives EBL Securities Limited regularly take initiatives to educate valued investors and make them informed.

  • Short Term- Existing investors; Employees of stakeholders; Teachers and trainers; Defence officers, judges, lawyers, journalists.
  • Midterm- School, college and university students; Civil/public servants, private sector officials; NGO employees, physicians, engineers; Middle income group.
  • Long Term- Home makers; Retired personnel, businessmen; Workers, Peasants; General public.
